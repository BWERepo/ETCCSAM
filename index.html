📖 ETCC Silent Auction Manager — User Manual

This application manages the silent auction for the East Tennessee Corvette Club (ETCC). Follow the six steps in order for each auction event.

Step 1 — Item Load

Scans the auction inbox at etccwebsite.auction@gmail.com for donation emails. Each email contains item details which are automatically extracted and loaded into the item table. Duplicate emails are skipped and each item receives a unique item number.

Step 2 — Create Bidding Sheets

Generates a printable bidding sheet for each item. Each sheet displays the item number, description, starting bid, bid increment, and a table of bid rows for bidders to fill in their bidder number and initials.

Step 3 — Bidder Registration

Register attendees as bidders. Import member numbers from a Club Express file. Send confirmation or information emails to individual bidders or to all bidders at once.

Step 4 — Auction Close

After the auction closes, enter the winning bidder number for each item. Use bulk entry mode to move through items quickly.

Step 5 — Winner Announcement

Generates a list of all winning bidders and items that can be announced to attendees or distributed digitally.

Step 6 — Payment & Pickup

Produces a payment report listing each winning bidder, the items they won, winning bid amounts, and payment details (Cash, Check, or Credit Card). Sortable by bidder number or last name.
